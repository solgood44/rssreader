Looking for **free audiobooks online** or a **free podcast library** you can open in a tab? You are not alone. Subscription apps are great, but plenty of legal, high-quality audio is already published as **public RSS feeds**—the same open format podcast apps use behind the scenes.

**Podcast library** collects many of those feeds in one searchable site and lets you **play them in the browser** without creating an account.

## Why “free” and “online” go together here

Most shows in the catalog stream from URLs listed in each publisher’s RSS feed. That means:

- **No subscription paywall on this site** — you browse and press play.
- **No app install** — works on laptop, tablet, or phone browsers.
- **Classic and public-domain audiobooks** often appear alongside modern podcast feeds, especially under **[Books](/category/books)**.

You are still listening to the publisher’s audio file; the site is a directory and player, not a file locker.

## Browser listening in three steps

1. Open a show page.
2. Press play on an episode.
3. Keep the tab open—the bottom player follows you around the site.

Favorites and resume progress stay on **this device only**. For a walkthrough with screenshots-level detail, see **[How to listen in your browser](/blog/how-to-listen-to-podcasts-in-your-browser)**.

## Honest limits (so expectations stay fair)

- **No offline mode** — you need a connection while streaming.
- **No cross-device sync** — your phone and laptop do not share history unless you use the same browser profile everywhere (uncommon).
- **Feed quality varies** — artwork, episode order, and link stability depend on each publisher.

Those tradeoffs keep the service simple and free to run.
