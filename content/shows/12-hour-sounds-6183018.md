"12 Hour Sounds" is a unique podcast that immerses its listeners in a world of diverse and continuous soundscapes. Each episode, exactly 12 hours long, is a meticulously crafted audio journey designed to accompany listeners through their day. The podcast features a variety of themes, ranging from the soothing sounds of nature, like forest ambience or ocean waves, to more dynamic urban environments, bustling cityscapes, or even the tranquil hum of a distant galaxy.
